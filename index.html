John Romero

The God Father of First Person Shooters

John Romero GDC 2023 with award

John Romero is a prominent Mexican-American video game designer, developer, and programmer known for his pivotal role in shaping the first-person shooter genre. As a co-founder of id Software, Romero played a crucial part in the creation and development of groundbreaking titles that revolutionized the gaming industry. He contributed significantly to iconic games such as Wolfenstein 3D, Doom, Doom 2, and Quake, leaving a lasting impact on the gaming landscape.
